Chocolate Cake- Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup Cocoa
  • 1 1/2 tsps baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsps baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  • 1. Heat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  • 2.Stir together 2 cups of sugar, 1 3/4 cups of flour, 3/4 cup of HERSHEY’S Cocoa, 1 1/2 tsps baking powder, 1 1/2 tsps baking soda and 1 tsp salt in large bowl.
  • 3. Add 2 eggs, 1 cup of milk, 1/2 cup of vegetable oil and 2 tsps vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes.
  • 4. Stir in 1 cup of boiling water (batter will be thin).
  • 5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  • 6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  • 7. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
  • 8. Frost with Perfectly Chocolate Frosting.
